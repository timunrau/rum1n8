r rum1n8

Pronounced "Ruminate" - to turn something over in the mind; to meditate or reflect on deeply.

A simple Bible memory app that gives you control of your data.

rum1n8 is intentionally simple, focused, and fast. It is built around first-letter typing, active recall, and spaced repetition so you can get into memorization quickly and keep your data under your control.

Open app

Why rum1n8 exists

I built rum1n8 to solve the frustrations I had with existing Bible memory apps. They were slow and bloated with too many features, had artificial paywalls and limits, and made it difficult or impossible to export my data.

01

Control.

Your verses stay on your device by default. You can back them up to a file you can read, or sync them with your own Google Drive or WebDAV server.

02

Free.

No artificial usage limits. No subscriptions. No paywalls.

03

Simple.

Open the app and use it. No signup, no login, no account required. No extra features, no extra clutter, and no bloat.

Built to last

Open source

Own your copy of the app.

Most apps only give you permission to use them, and if the owner stops supporting them, you can lose access. rum1n8 is self-hostable and released under the MIT License, so you can copy the whole app, run it yourself, and not depend on me to keep it online forever.
