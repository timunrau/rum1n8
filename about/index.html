r rum1n8

verb roo-muh-nate

Ruminate: to turn something over in the mind.

That's all this app does. Memorize Scripture simply, with your data under your control.

Open app

How it works

1

Add a verse

Just type the reference — no finicky book-chapter-verse pickers. The text is fetched automatically.

2

Learn it

Type the first letter of each word to build recall through three stages — Learn, Memorize, Master.

3

Review daily

Spaced repetition surfaces each verse at the right time based on how well you know it.

Why rum1n8 exists

I built rum1n8 because existing Bible memory apps got in the way more than they helped — too many features, too many limits, and no way to export your data.

01

Sovereign.

Your verses stay on your device by default. You can back them up to a readable file, or sync them with your own Google Drive or WebDAV server.

02

Simple.

No account, no setup, no extras. Open the app and you're memorizing in seconds.

03

Free.

No ads, no artificial usage limits, no subscriptions, and no paywalls. And it's open source.
