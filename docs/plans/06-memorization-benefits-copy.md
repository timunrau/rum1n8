## Second page copy

I know. Memorization sounds like homework.

But this simple daily practice unlocks real growth in your walk with God.

## It Changes Your Behaviour

Everyone's behaviour is influenced by something. The culture around you. The shows you watch. The feeds you scroll. The influencers you follow.

Or God's word.

Jesus says:

> "For out of the overflow of the heart, the mouth speaks." (Luke 6:45)

That is why God commands us to meditate on his word day and night.

> "This Book of the Law must not depart from your mouth; **meditate on it day and night, so that you may be careful to do everything written in it.** For then you will prosper and succeed in all you do." (Joshua 1:8)

See also: [Deuteronomy 6:6-9](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/DEU.6.6-9.BSB), [Deuteronomy 11:18-19](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/DEU.11.18-19.BSB), [Psalm 1:1-3](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/PSA.1.1-3.BSB), [Psalm 19:7](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/PSA.19.7.BSB), [Psalm 119:11](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/PSA.119.11.BSB), [Psalm 119:15-16](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/PSA.119.15-16.BSB), [Psalm 119:97-99](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/PSA.119.97-99.BSB), [Proverbs 4:20-23](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/PRO.4.20-23.BSB), [Proverbs 7:1-3](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/PRO.7.1-3.BSB), [John 8:31-32](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/JHN.8.31-32.BSB), [Romans 12:2](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/ROM.12.2.BSB), [2 Timothy 3:16-17](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/2TI.3.16-17.BSB), [Hebrews 4:12](https://www.bible.com/bible/3034/HEB.4.12.BSB)

## It Changes Your Desires

Delight trains desire.

> "Delight yourself in the LORD, and He will give you the desires of your heart." (Psalm 37:4)

When you meditate on God's word, your heart starts wanting what God wants.

## It Reduces Anxiety

Anxiety rehearses the "what ifs".

Scripture memorization helps you rehearse God's truth.

> "Be anxious for nothing, but in everything, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which surpasses all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus. Finally, brothers, whatever is true, whatever is honorable, whatever is right, whatever is pure, whatever is lovely, whatever is admirable--if anything is excellent or praiseworthy--**think on these things**." (Philippians 4:6-8)

That leads to peace:

> "You will keep in perfect peace the steadfast of mind, because he trusts in You." (Isaiah 26:3)

## It Leads To Answered Prayer And Much Fruit

Check out this promise Jesus made:

> "If you remain in Me and My words remain in you, ask whatever you wish, and it will be done for you. This is to My Father's glory, that you bear much fruit, proving yourselves to be My disciples." (John 15:7-8)

Who doesn't want answered prayer and much fruit? Jesus connects both to His words remaining in us. So get His words in you.

## Just Do One Verse

You do not need a complicated system.

Start with one verse. Practice for a few minutes. Come back tomorrow.

rum1n8 makes that easy.
